Workflow

Discovery

Using our multidisciplinary team, we will take the time to understand your proposal and ensure that our vision is the same as yours.

Preliminary Design

Using peer reviewed schematics, we'll provide accurate diagrams to ensure for precise modelling of the overall system. For us, this is a fundamental part of the project.

Modelling

Through verification of our highly experienced Engineers using cost effective rapid prototyping services. The components are placed on the board ready for routing. ParaV's own compliance lab is used for all verification and so reduces time to market.

Design Completion

To maintain our high standards, the design is tested and verified under your specifications to ensure that the product fits your need.

Production

We are partners with a reputable integrated electronics manufacturer in the UK. Having worked on many different projects covering a multitude of industries; we can support you in any type of PCB assembly, both large and small.

Project Completion

We will provide all project results, documents & data and will be at your support anytime in the future.
